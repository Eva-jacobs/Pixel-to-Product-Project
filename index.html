Why Pixel to Product?

Pixel to Product isn’t just a bootcamp—it’s a bold step toward closing the gender gap in tech. This all-female UI/UX bootcamp is built to empower women with the skills they need to become world-class designers. Founded with the vision of bridging the gender gap in tech, Niah offers a 4-month UI/UX design training program tailored for women with little or no tech background. Our curriculum is designed to take you from beginner to job-ready, equipping you with the skills, confidence, and portfolio needed to succeed in the fast-growing field of design.

Ask Away! We've Got You

Who is the Pixel to Product bootcamp for?

The Pixel to Product bootcamp is designed for women who want to transition into tech, regardless of their background. If you’re a student, recent graduate, early/mid-career professional, this program will equip you with the skills to launch a career in UI/UX design.

What do I need to have to make the most of the bootcamp?

You will need to have a functioning laptop, access to the internet, a note pad, a curious mind and a willingness to learn. No prior tech experience is required. You will have an amazing time and we cannot wait to onboard you.

How much does it cost, and are there payment plans?

The bootcamp costs N30,000 and there are no installment plans. After payment, send a screenshot of the proof to our email address.

Are the classes onsite or virtual?

All the classes will be held virtually and are recorded so you will always have access to re-watch at your convenience.
